Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 15 Aug 2024

Welcome to Grow Tube . This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

We do not collect personal data beyond what is necessary to provide and improve our services. We may collect the following information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our app, such as the time spent on the app, the videos you watch, and the features you use.
  • Interaction Data: Data on your interactions with videos, including play and pause events, as well as preferences and interests based on your viewing history.

1.2 Tracking Technologies

We use the following tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Session Tracking: We monitor the time spent on the app to improve our services and user experience.
  • Video Watch Time: We track how long you watch videos to help improve content recommendations and user engagement.
  • Interest Tracking: We record your interactions with content, such as likes, shares, and comments, to better understand your preferences and provide personalized recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Improve Our Services: Analyzing app usage and video watch time helps us understand user behavior and improve the app's functionality.
  • To Personalize Content: Your interaction data helps us recommend videos and features that match your interests.
  • To Ensure a Better User Experience: Session and interaction tracking enable us to provide a more engaging and tailored experience.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use encryption and secure storage practices to safeguard your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or with trusted partners who assist us in operating our app and providing services. These partners are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your data for the purposes outlined in this policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of tracking features by adjusting your app settings or contacting us directly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant updates. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
